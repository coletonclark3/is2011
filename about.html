Facts From My Mission

I served my mission in two different countries, including various cultures and backgrounds. I spoke 5 languages among the Navajo Tribe, Zuni tribe, and Joao Pessoa, Brazil.

I served with 4 mission presidents, 22 different companions, various areas, and preached the Gospel of Jesus Christ in English, Navajo, Zuni, Spanish, Sign-language, and Portuguese.

I spent 6 weeks in the MTC from home on zoom calls, 13 months on the Indian Reservations, and 11 months in Brazil.
