Irmao Manoel, A Miracle

One day we were walking to an appointment and felt prompted to stop and knock on one specific door. Irmao Manoel opened the door, shirtless, and welcomed us in. Miracles happened daily leading to his baptism!

"Remember the worth of souls is great in the sight of God;" - D&C 18:10

Miracles happen every day. It's amazing to recognize the Lord's hand in His work!

As I reflect on my mission, I am deeply humbled and extremely grateful for how much the Lord has blessed me. I feel so blessed to have helped play a small role in Irmao Manoel's spiritual journey along the covenant path, and I hope to see him again in a few years when the temple will be dedicated in Joao Pessoa.
